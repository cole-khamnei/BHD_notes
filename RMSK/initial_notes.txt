vasculitis:
	- symptoms:
		- fever
		- myalgia
		- athralgia
		- fatigue
	- pathogenesis: WBCs in vessel walls
	- palpable purpura: non-blanching raised purpura
	- tx: steroids or cyclophosphamide

	- types:
		- large vessel:
			- temporal (giant cell) arteritis:
				- epidiomiology: asian female
				- pathogenesis:
					- inflammation of temporal artery
				- sx:
					- headache
					- jaw pain (chewing)
					- opthalmic artery occlusion -> blindness
				- dx:
					- high ESR
					- biopsy: granulomas
				- tx: steroids (asap cuz blindnessx)
			
			- takayasu's arteritis:
				- epidiomiology: asian, mostly female (90%)
				- pathogenesis:
					- granulomatous thickening in aortic arch + branches
				- sx:
					- weak/pulseless limb
					- BP difference between limbs
					- bruits over arterities.
				- dx: high ESR + aortagram
				- tx: steroids

		- medium vessel:
			- kawasaki disease:
				- epidiomiology: asian child
				- pathogenesis:
				- sx:
					- diffuse red rash -> desquamation
					- strawberry tongue + lips
					- fever, lymphadenopathy
					- thombrocytosis
					- arthritis
					- myocarditis
					- risk: coronary aneurysm -> rupture = MI
				- dx:
				- tx: aspirin + IVIG

			- buerger's disease:
				- epidiomiology: male smokers
				- pathogenesis:
				- sx:
					- smoker's hands/feet
					- gangrene -> autoamputation
					- superficial nodular phlebitis (tender)
					- raynaud's phenomenon
					- segmental thrombosing vasculitis
				- dx:
				- tx: smoking cessation

			- polyarteritis nodosa:
				- epidiomiology:
				- pathogenesis:
					- hepatitis B
					- type III hypersensitivity - immune complexes
					- biopsy: transmural inflammation - fibrinoid necrosis
				- sx:
					- aneurysms: renal, liver, or mesenteric arteries
						- rosary sign
					- neuro deficits
					- skin: nodules + purpura
					- prerenal failure
				- dx: angiogram + biopsy
				- tx: steroids + cyclophosphamide

		- small vessel:
			- henoch-schÃ¶nlein purpura:
				- epidiomiology: child (most peds vascuitis)
				- pathogenesis:
					- post-URI
					- IgA + C3 deposition
				- sx:
					- palpable purpura on butt/legs
					- GI: pain + melena
					- renal: IgA nephropathy
				- dx: biopsy
				- tx: self limited, if severe: steroids + cyclophosphamide

			- ANCA diseases:
				- sx:
					- lungs
					- kidneys:
						- crestentic rapidly progressive glomerulonephritis (RPGN)
						- pauci immune: negative immunoflouroscence
						- proteinuria + hematuria
					- tx: steroids + cyclophosphamide
				
				- types:
					- churg-strauss:
						- pathogenesis: granulomas + necrosis
						- sx:
							- asthma
							- sinusitis
							- neuropathy
							- palpable purpura
						- dx: 
							- p-ANCA
							- high IgE
							- eosinophilia

					- microscopic polyangiitis:
						- pathogenesis: no granulomas
						- sx:
							- hemoptysis
							- purpura
						- dx: p-ANCA: myeloperoxidase (MPO)

					- wegener's granulomatosis:
						- pathogenesis: granulomas
						- sx:
							- sinusitis
							- otitis media
							- hemoptysis
							- upper + lower airway disease
							- purpura
						- dx: biopsy + c-ANCA: proteinase 3 (PR3)
						

	- scarlet fever:
		- sx:
			- fever, pharyngitis
			- diffuse red papule rash -> desquamation
			- sand paper skin
			- seeded strawberry tongue

	- reye's syndrome:
		- sx:
			- encephalopathy
			- fatty liver failure
			- vomiting
			- confusion
			- seizures
			- coma
		- pathogenesis:
			- follows virus: flue, varicella
			- diffuse mitochondrial insult
			- aspirin use for kids EXCEPT kawasaki


	- goodpasture's syndrome:
		- pathogenesis:
			- type II hypersensitivity
			- antibody to collagen
				- alpha-3 chain of IV
				- anti-GBM
				- anti-alveoli
			- biospy: linear immunoflouroscence 
				- IgG + C3 deposition
		- sx:
			- hemoptysis
			- nephritis syndrome
		- epidiomiology: young man
